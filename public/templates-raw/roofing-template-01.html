05 — STORM DAMAGE

We Handle the Insurance So You Don't Have To

If your roof was damaged by hail, wind, or a storm, your homeowner's insurance likely covers it. Most {{CITY}} homeowners do not realize this — and many roofing companies do not tell them. We do.

1. Free Storm Inspection

HAAG-certified inspection with complete photo records matching actual adjuster requirements.

2. Claim Filing

We support your claim submission and meet the insurance adjuster on site to secure full coverage.

Signs of Storm Damage

Missing or visibly displaced shingles
Excessive dark granules accumulating in gutters
Asphalt dents, bruising, or missing round spots (hail)
Wind lifting roof edge flashing around vents/chimneys
Water marks appearing unexpectedly on ceiling boards
